I strive to be an intentional team player first, an immersive formidable force, engineering & design enthusiast with a self-starter attitude second; seeking to understand all aspects — ethical, product, design, business, technical, and most importantly the mission of applications on which I work — so that I can be in tight alignment with the vision and most effective with my gumption.

With over half a decade+ of being an engineering lead in healthcare, I've found creating solutions that significantly enhance the health and quality of life for other humans to be the most rewarding archetype of being an engineer.

Passionate to build on my vocation as an engineer, and hankering for always learning skillsets anew. Being inexorably keen of growing the landscape of ways in which I add value, always seeking avenues to solve urgent problems for organizational leaders with acute attention to detail.

Amongst the innumerable captivations I could make profession but intentionally choose not to: talking to gifted souls by digesting what they've written, having a full-time job as a plant dad, being underwater exploring our oceans and making sea life friends, volunteering at community botanical gardens, curating art dubbed as experiences, working with stained glass and taking photographs, spending hours lost in astounding artwork by the greatest artists humanity has ever seen — all while my brain is philosophizing our human experience the entire time to background appreciation of music.
